Welcome to Mobile Legends

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) is a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Moonton. Since its release in 2016, the game has gained a huge following and has become one of the most played mobile games in the world. With the growing popularity of MLBB, it was only a matter of time before the game became a major player in the esports industry.


In recent years, the esports scene for MLBB has grown significantly. The game's developer, Moonton, has made significant investments in the esports system, including organizing major tournaments and creating a professional league structure. The MLBB Professional League (MPL) is a league system that consists of various regional leagues in Southeast Asia, China, and Brazil. Each league has its own set of teams, and the top teams from each league compete in the Mobile Legends: Bang Bang World Championship.

The MLBB World Championship is the highest level of competition in the game, and it offers a massive prize pool. The most recent World Championship in 2021 had a prize pool of over $1.2 million, with the winning team taking home $500,000. The tournament was held in Singapore and featured some of the best MLBB players from around the world.

In addition to the MPL and the World Championship, Moonton also hosts various other tournaments and events throughout the year, including the Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Southeast Asia Cup (MSC) and the Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Professional League Invitational (MPLI).

The growing popularity of MLBB esports has also led to the development of a strong esports community. Fans of the game can follow their favorite teams and players, watch live streams and videos, and participate in online discussions and forums. The game's official website and social media channels provide regular updates and news about upcoming tournaments and events, as well as interviews with players and teams.

The success of MLBB esports is a testament to the growing importance of mobile gaming in the esports industry. With the rapid growth of mobile gaming and the increasing popularity of games like MLBB, it is clear that mobile esports are here to stay. As more and more players and fans get involved in the esports scene, we can expect to see even more growth and development in the coming years.

In conclusion, the esports system for Mobile Legends: Bang Bang has become a major player in the esports industry. With significant investments from the game's developer, Moonton, and the growing popularity of the game among players and fans, MLBB esports has established itself as a major force in the mobile gaming world. As the industry continues to grow and evolve, we can expect to see even more exciting developments in the world of MLBB esports.
